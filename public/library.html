About Library:

A library is a collection of resources, materials, and services that are organized and maintained for use by a community. Libraries can be physical buildings, virtual spaces, or both, and can include a variety of materials such as books, periodicals, newspapers, films, and morep

Here are some things to know about libraries:

Purpose

Libraries provide access to information and knowledge, and can support research, recreation, and other needs of the community.

Staff

Libraries are staffed by librarians and other personnel who are trained to help users find what they need.

Organization

Libraries use systems to organize materials, such as the Dewey Decimal System, which divides materials into 10 main subjects, then 10 smaller sections within each subject, and so on.

Access

Libraries can provide physical access to materials, or digital access through online catalogs.

History

The first libraries were archives of clay tablets in Sumer, dating back to 2600 BC. The first subscription library in Colonial British America was established in 1731 by Benjamin Franklin and his friends.
